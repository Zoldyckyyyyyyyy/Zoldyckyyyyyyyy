Ivan Alexis Escalona

I am Ivan Alexis D. Escalona, I'm from Brgy. Teraza, Palo, Leyte. I was born at Palo Maternity house, on the 23rd day of January. I have two siblings, my sister and my younger brother. I graduated my elementary at Castilla Elementary School, finished my junior high school at Anahaway National High School.I was raised in a house where positivity is often seen. I grew up dreaming of things that is quite unrealistic, but expanding my knowledge is the substitution to attain such dreams that for me is possible, and that is being a biologist.

Since I was a child, I was fond of watching dinosaur's and archeologic documentaries. I knew at that moment that I will adore and love science. In my elementary and High School experiences, I mostly excel at science and math based subjects, which concludes why I like science. My dream job as of now, is to be a biologist, to be specific, a Marine Biologist. I want to work in the ocean, and study various living organism that inhabits that vast oceans.

I also entertain myself in reading books, especially novels, I also write poems that captures the essence of both reality and fantasy. I mostly write such literary work whenever I find myself being motivated by someone or something. It is my way to escape reality, and live in my mind where freedom and peace can be seen and felt. I am the type of person that's quite loud and energetic, but sometimes fainthearted.
